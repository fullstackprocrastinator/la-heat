The LA Heat Experience

Welcome to LA Heat, a premium mobile sauna experience housed in a beautifully crafted, traditional-style wagon hut. We bring the timeless tradition of heat therapy to the modern world, offering a unique sanctuary for your physical and mental well-being.

Whether you're looking to soothe aching muscles after a heavy workout or simply want a quiet escape from the daily grind, our wood-fired sauna provides the perfect environment to disconnect and recharge.

  • Recover

    Heat therapy increases blood flow, helping to flush lactic acid from muscles and accelerating post-workout recovery.

  • Relax

    Step away from screens and stress. The gentle crackle of the fire and the enveloping heat melt away tension.

  • Unwind

    Regular sauna use is linked to better sleep, improved cardiovascular health, and a profound sense of mental clarity.
